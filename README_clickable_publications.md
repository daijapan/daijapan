# Dave Ishii

**Founder, Kiara Inc.**\
**Mathematical AI Systems Research**

> *Building the mathematical foundations that make AI universally
> accessible, predictable, and trustworthy.*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Research Vision

Artificial intelligence should not merely automate work---it should
systematically expand human capability.

My long-term research aims to establish a mathematical theory that
explains:

-   What AI can make accessible
-   What AI cannot
-   Why these limits exist
-   How these limits can be expanded

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Mathematical Accessibility

Understanding accessibility as a mathematical object rather than merely
a software feature.

**Authors:** Rizwan Jahangir, Daisuke Ishii\
**Venue:** arXiv, 2026
