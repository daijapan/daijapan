## Mathematical AI

### Explainable PQC

A research program for interpreting post-quantum cryptographic assumptions through computational hardness, lattice geometry, combinatorial structures, and empirical experiments.

### Lean 4 Verification for Language Models

A formal verification direction studying whether safety properties of language-model components can be represented and checked in dependent type theory.

### Accessibility Capability Boundary

A mathematical and experimental framework for evaluating AI-generated accessibility systems.
