## Research Vision

Artificial intelligence should not merely automate work. It should systematically expand human capability.

My long-term research aims to establish a mathematical theory that explains:

- what AI can make accessible,
- what AI cannot make accessible,
- why these limits exist,
- and how those limits can be expanded.

> **Accessibility** is treated here as the set of human capabilities made reachable through AI.

### 2. Reliable Mathematical AI

Replacing heuristic engineering with AI systems whose behavior can be described, bounded, and verified mathematically.

**Representative work**
